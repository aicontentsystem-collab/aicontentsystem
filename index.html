How it works
From trending video to
published content in minutes.
01
Compliance first
Every video passes through four automated checks before processing begins. License verification, channel trust, content policy, and AI legal risk assessment. Zero copyright violations.
02
Trend detection
Real-time monitoring of 47 keywords across 3 niches. Videos scored by view velocity, engagement rate, and trending momentum. Only the top 10 make it through each day.
03
AI clip selection
Anthropic Claude reads full video transcripts and identifies the 3 best clips based on hook strength, emotional arc, pacing, and predicted completion rate.
04
Automated editing
FFmpeg converts each clip to 9:16 vertical format using split-blur background fill. OpenAI Whisper generates word-level captions burned directly into the video.
05
TikTok publishing
Official TikTok Content Posting API. Every clip published with AI-generated captions and hashtags at the optimal time for the target audience. Full compliance with platform policies.
06
Blog monetization
Every video transcript becomes a 1000-word SEO article published to WordPress within 30 minutes. AdSense ads placed strategically. Traffic compounds over time.
